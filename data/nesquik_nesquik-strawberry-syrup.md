#  NESQUIK Strawberry Syrup 
510 ml
Wake up your milk with NESQUIK Strawberry Syrup (510 ml) - NEW recipe & look; with the same great taste! Proudly prepared in Canada and made with no artificial flavours, colours or sweeteners. It’s also enriched with iron to promote the maintenance of your child’s good health. Enjoy as part of a balanced breakfast or afternoon snack, with the taste your child will love. It’s quick and easy: simply squeeze, stir, and enjoy for a delicious glass of fruity, strawberry-flavoured milk. NESQUIK is the fun & delicious way to enjoy the nutritious benefits of milk!
