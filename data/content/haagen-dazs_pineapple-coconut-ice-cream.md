[Description](https://www.haagen-dazs.ca/en/haagen-dazs/pineapple-coconut-ice-cream#3736068183)
Indulge in the irresistible taste of HÄAGEN-DAZS® Pineapple Coconut Ice Cream. Since 1960, our passion has taken us on a journey to transform only the finest ingredients into a simply extraordinary ice cream experience. Made with 100% Canadian dairy, this tropical-inspired treat combines tangy pineapple pieces and coconut flavour with pure, sweet cream. Every bite of this treat will inspire an exotic getaway.
[Features and Benefits](https://www.haagen-dazs.ca/en/haagen-dazs/pineapple-coconut-ice-cream#3002636260)
  * An exotic combination of tangy pineapple, coconut and pure, sweet cream.
  * Our ice cream is made with 100% Canadian dairy.
  * Each tub is finished with a quality seal to guarantee freshness.
  * HÄAGEN-DAZS® has been perfecting premium ice cream since 1960.
  * Blends perfectly into a homemade Pina Colada or tropical beverage.
